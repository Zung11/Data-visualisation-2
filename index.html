WORLD'S SUICIDE RATE
2009 - 2019

Overview

In 2019, suicide was responsible for
1.4% of all deaths
taking away 800,000 lives
which is one person every 40 seconds

Where?

Regions of highest suicide rate

In 2019, out of the 10 countries with highest suicide rate,6 are from Africa

Lesotho's suicide rate is 87.49, which is almost 10x higher than the global's average of 9 per 100,000

Suicide rate over 10 years

The continent of Africa has the highest suicide rate, 1.6 times higher than the global average

Generally speaking, countries where people earn less have higher suicide rates than countries with high incomes. This explains the decrease in suicide rates in Africa over the last 10 years

Who?

Suicide rate by age and gender

Suicide rate increases exponentially as age inceases due to a number of reasons:

- Grief over lost loved ones
- Loss of self-sufficiency
- Chronic illness and pain
- Cognitive impairment
- Financial troubles

Not so fun fact:
On average, women tend to have more sucuidal thoughts, but men are way more likely to commit suicide
